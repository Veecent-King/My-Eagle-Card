EAGLE

Eagles are one of the biggest birds on the planet. They are at the top of the food chain with eyesight around 5 times better than the human's vision. There are more than 60 eagle species, Eagles can see up to 3 kilometers away.

  • Scientific Name: Accipitridae
  • Eagle specie: There are over 60 species of eagle
  • Average size: height is 28 to 38 inches and their wingspan is 5.5 to 8 feet.
  • Average length:1.8-2.3m
  • Average Lifespan: 20-30 Years
  • Habitat: nests on high cliffs and in tall trees near water bodies like streams, lakes, and rivers

Eagles are generally larger and more powerful than hawks and may resemble a vulture in build and flight characteristics, but they have a fully feathered (often crested) head and strong feet equipped with great curved talons. Most species subsist mainly on live prey, which they generally capture on the ground.
